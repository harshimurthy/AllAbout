Help Women Lead Founder

A campaign led in India to empower women and encourage underprivileged girls to continue studying
01

Objective

The campaign involved 360 girls from different cities educating 15,000 girls about about the importance of education. The campaign was a huge success which inspired young girls and empowered women

02

Spread

HWL is spread over in 5 major cities of India including Bangalore, Bombay, Chennai, Delhi and Calcutta. The best way to spread awareness was through facebook HWL Facebook page

03

The feedback obtained out of the campaign was outstanding and as the campaign spread, more and more women got involved and voiced their thoughts and worked towards their asporations.
